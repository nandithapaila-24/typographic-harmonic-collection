A New York Day

Today, I walked to Central Park. I biked around and fed the pigeons. I sat on a bench and read a book I have been trying to finish for ages. I saw the cutest dog wearing a jacket and shoes. The fall leaves are turning various shades of red, orange and green. There was a slight, autumn chill in the air. It was a good day.
